nanogallery2


Everything you need to add beautiful, modern and high quality photo galleries to your website.




gallery_made_with_nanogallery2



















Focused on providing the best experience to your visitors, nanogallery2 is a highly customizable javascript library.







Beautiful Photo Gallery

Style the layout to suit your personal needs, and add eye-catching effects.

Great Photo Viewer

Lightbox for highlighting your photos and for handy navigation.

Visual Builder

To generate your galleries without coding. You can also use HTML markup or javascript.






ONE TOOL = UNLIMITED GALLERIES BE CREATIVE







For Developers

If you are a developer, you may use nanogallery2 with javascript.

For Designers

If you are a designer, you'll love to use nanogallery2 just with HTML markup.

For Everyone

nanogallery2 is designed for everyone as the easiest and fastest way to create engaging photo galleries.

 



Display content from your Web Server, Flickr or Google Photos
nanoPhotosProvider2: add-on to generate thumbnails automatically
Build a gallery in minutes with the Visual Builder
Full featured documentation with samples
Tons of new possibilities since the popular nanoGALLERY
Also available without any installation: nanogallery2 portable

nanogallery2 licensing - clear and simple





nanogallery2 has a dual-license model:
- a GPLv3 license for non-commercial use,
- and a proprietary commercial license.


  compare nanogallery2 licenses




 

Commercial license

Open source license



If you run a business and are using nanogallery2 in a revenue-generating product, it makes business sense to sponsor this development: it ensures the project that your product relies on stays healthy and actively maintained.
Note: if you have a nanoGALLERY license, there's no upgrade fee.

  get your license

For personal or open source projects, you may use nanogallery2 for free. In this case, nanogallery2 is licensed under GPLv3 license, which means that your project must also be licensed under GPLv3. If this cannot apply to your project, the purchase of a commercial license is required.
